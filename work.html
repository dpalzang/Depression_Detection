    • Depression Detection

    In 2022, it was estimated that there were more than 300 million depressed people worldwide, or 5% of the world's population. The National Mental Health Survey 2022 in India found that 1 in 3 Indians has depression and the cases stand at over 56 million and that nearly 15% of Indian adults require active intervention for one or more mental health issues. About 800,000 people die by suicide worldwide every year. 164,033 Indians committed suicide in 2021. People aged 15 to 49 being the most affected. It is the second most common reason for death among people in this age group. However, not much importance is given to mental health awareness or means to help patients battle depression. This depression detection system aims to diagnose and identify the type of depression (anxiety, post-traumatic stress disorder (PTSD), or bipolar disorder) and suggests professional help.
